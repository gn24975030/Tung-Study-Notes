# Topic 2: Interdependence and Gains from Trade（第 2 課：相互依賴與貿易利益）

**Course (課程)**: Principles of Economics 2026  
**Topic (主題)**: Topic 2: Interdependence and Gains from Trade（相互依賴與貿易利益）

本課要學的是：當兩個人（或兩個國家）能彼此交易時，為什麼「比較利益」會讓貿易創造雙贏，且大家都能變得更好。

---

## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 International Trade（國際貿易）

International economics deals with the economic interdependence among nations.  
國際經濟學研究各國之間的經濟互相依賴。

Two basic questions of international trade theory:  
國際貿易理論有兩個基本問題：

1. What is the basis for trade? Why do people / countries trade?  
   貿易的基礎是什麼？為什麼人／國家會貿易？

2. What is the pattern of trade? What determines which good a country exports and which good it imports?  
   貿易的型態是什麼？一國出口哪些、進口哪些？

---

## 🧩 Why Trade?（為什麼要貿易？）

Basic reasons countries engage in trade:  
各國進行貿易的基本原因：

- Access to resources they lack, or the goods are expensive to produce locally (cheaper to import).  
  取得缺乏的資源，或是本地生產很貴（所以進口比較便宜）。

- Can specialize in producing goods and services where they have comparative advantage, and higher production can lead to better efficiency (lower costs).  
  能在比較利益存在的領域專業化生產，並讓更高產出帶來更好的效率（較低成本）。

- Market expansion and economic growth.  
  市場擴大與經濟成長。

---

## A Simple Example（簡單例子：用兩人兩商品理解貿易）
Use a 2-person (Farmer & Rancher), 2-good (Potatoes & Meat) economy to illustrate comparative advantage and why trade can be mutually beneficial.  
用兩個人（Farmer 與 Rancher）與兩種商品（Potatoes 與 Meat）來說明比較利益原則。

### Setup（設定）
- Two persons: Farmer (F) and Rancher (R)  
  兩人：Farmer（F）與 Rancher（R）

- Two goods: Potatoes (P) and Meat (M)  
  兩商品：Potatoes（P）與 Meat（M）

- They both like Potatoes and Meat  
  兩人都喜歡 P 與 M

- Each person works 8 hours (= 480 min.) a day  
  每天工作 8 小時＝480 分鐘

### Productivity（生產力）
Farmer: Potatoes 15 min./oz; Meat 60 min./oz.  
Farmer 的生產力：P 15 分鐘/盎司、M 60 分鐘/盎司。

Rancher: Potatoes 10 min./oz; Meat 20 min./oz.  
Rancher 的生產力：P 10 分鐘/盎司、M 20 分鐘/盎司。

---

## Summary of Productivity & Output（生產力與最大產出總結）
Farmer can produce 32 oz potatoes or 8 oz meat in 8 hours.  
Farmer 在 8 小時內最多：32 盎司馬鈴薯或 8 盎司肉。

Rancher can produce 48 oz potatoes or 24 oz meat in 8 hours.  
Rancher 在 8 小時內最多：48 盎司馬鈴薯或 24 盎司肉。

---

## Farmer’s PPF & Slope（農夫的 PPF 與斜率）
Assume Farmer can switch between producing P and producing M at a constant rate, so the PPF is a straight line.  
假設 Farmer 在 P 與 M 之間能以固定比例切換，使 PPF 為直線。

The slope represents opportunity cost:  
Farmer 的 PPF 斜率可以理解成「機會成本」：

- 1 oz of meat costs 4 oz of potatoes (1M requires giving up 4P).  
  1 盎司肉的「成本」是 4 盎司馬鈴薯（1M 需要放棄 4P）。

---

## Rancher’s PPF & Slope（牧場主的 PPF 與斜率）
Rancher's PPF is also a straight line; the switching ratio is 2:1.  
Rancher 的 PPF 也為直線，切換比例是 2:1。

- 1 oz of meat costs 2 oz of potatoes.  
  1 盎司肉的成本是 2 盎司馬鈴薯（1M 需要放棄 2P）。

---

## Without Trade（不貿易）
Without trade, they can only consume what they produce individually.  
不貿易時，每個人只能消費自己生產的東西。

Suppose choose:  
假設選擇：

- Farmer \((P_F, M_F) = (16, 4)\)  
- Rancher \((P_R, M_R) = (24, 12)\)

Total output = \((40, 16)\)  
總產出＝\((40, 16)\)

---

## Absolute Advantage（絕對利益）
The producer who can make a good using less input (or makes more with the same input) has an absolute advantage.  
能用較少投入生產更多（或用更少投入生產同樣產量）的人有「絕對利益」。

In this example, Rancher has an absolute advantage in both potatoes and meat.  
本例中 Rancher 在馬鈴薯與肉都更強。

---

## Comparative Advantage（比較利益）
Comparative advantage is based on opportunity cost: lower opportunity cost means comparative advantage.  
比較利益看的是「機會成本」：機會成本較低的人在該商品上有比較利益。

Opportunity cost is what you give up to obtain that good.  
機會成本＝你為了得到某樣東西必須放棄什麼。

---

## Opportunity Cost（機會成本：用另一種商品表示）
### Meat 的機會成本（用 P 表示）
Opportunity cost of meat is in terms of potatoes.  
肉的機會成本（以馬鈴薯衡量）：

- Farmer: gives up 4 oz of potatoes for 1 more oz of meat.  
  Farmer：多 1 盎司肉 → 放棄 4 盎司馬鈴薯

- Rancher: gives up 2 oz of potatoes for 1 more oz of meat.  
  Rancher：多 1 盎司肉 → 放棄 2 盎司馬鈴薯

### Potato 的機會成本（用 M 表示）
Opportunity cost of potatoes is in terms of meat.  
馬鈴薯的機會成本（以肉衡量）：

- Farmer: gives up 1/4 oz of meat.  
  Farmer：多 1 盎司馬鈴薯 → 放棄 1/4 盎司肉

- Rancher: gives up 1/2 oz of meat.  
  Rancher：多 1 盎司馬鈴薯 → 放棄 1/2 盎司肉

---

## Specialization and Trade（專業分工與貿易）
Since Farmer has the lower opportunity cost in potato production:  
因為 Farmer 在生產馬鈴薯的機會成本較低：

- Specialize in producing potatoes and trade them for meat.  
  他應該專業生產馬鈴薯並與 Rancher 交換肉

Since Rancher has the lower opportunity cost in meat production:  
因為 Rancher 在生產肉的機會成本較低：

- Specialize in producing meat and trade them for potatoes.  
  她應該專業生產肉並與 Farmer 交換馬鈴薯

If specialization increases total output, both can be better off.  
若專業分工能增加總產出，雙方都能更好。

---

## Terms of Trade（貿易條件：交換率）
Trade needs an exchange rate (terms of trade) that both would accept.  
貿易要發生需要「雙方都接受」的交換率。

One possible terms of trade is 3P = 1M.  
投影片提供一個可行的交換率：3P = 1M。

---

## Gains from Trade（貿易利益）
After specialization and trade, both are better off than before.  
專業分工＋交換後，兩人都會比不貿易時更好。

---

## Summary（總結）
- Absolute advantage is about producing more with less input.  
  絕對利益（Absolute advantage）看「投入是否較少」。

- Opportunity cost is the trade-off you give up.  
  機會成本（Opportunity cost）是你必須放棄的取捨。

- Comparative advantage depends on lower opportunity cost.  
  比較利益（Comparative advantage）看「機會成本誰更低」。

- Gains from trade are based on comparative advantage, not absolute advantage.  
  貿易利益源自比較利益，不是絕對利益。
